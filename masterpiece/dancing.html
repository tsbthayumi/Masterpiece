Dancing

In general, dance is a branch of art that expresses beauty, expression, and certain meanings through the medium of body movements which are arranged and demonstrated in such a way as to provide a pleasant appearance and experience or create new horizons for the audience. Dance can be performed singly, in pairs, in groups or colossally.Â 

Dance also acts as a performing art or is often referred to as a theatrical art. According to Susanne K. Langer, an American art philosopher, philosophically, dance as a spectator art is a change in the birth of the human inner process to be seen by oneself and by others.
